The project

Welcome to our project, where we explore the fascinating world of art through the lens of digital humanities. Our website delves into the rich cultural heritage of artworks, examining how symbols and genres evolve over time. By leveraging the power of Wikidata and Semantic Web technologies, we uncover hidden patterns and insights that reveal the dynamic nature of artistic expression.

Symbols

In this section, we are about to uncover the most common symbols in 2D artworks attributed to artists. By analyzing the Wikidata dataset, we identify the recurring motifs that have captivated artists across the centuries. From the Medieval religious symbols to the modern abstract expressions of the 20th century, we explore how symbols have been used to convey meaning and tell stories. Our analysis reveals the frequency of these symbols over time, providing a unique perspective on the evolution of artistic expression.

Genres

In this part of our project, we shift our focus to the genres that have shaped the artistic landscape. We examine the distribution of artworks across different artistic movements. By analyzing the frequency of genres within a movement, we uncover the dominant styles that have defined the artistic identity of each period. We also explore the ratio of opposing genres, such as landscape and portrait, to understand how these contrasts have influenced the development of art throughout history.
