Here are our research questions

Click to delve into each one

01 How do the subjects of artworks change through time?

What are the most common symbols in Wikidata 2D artworks attributed to an artist?


How often do they appear through the centuries?

02 How does the distribution of the artworks' genre change in different artistic movements?

What are the artistic movements with the highest number of notable works present in wikidata and how are they distributed in time?


Taking couples of opposed genres (e.g. landscape/portrait), how does the frequency of each genre of the couple, i.e. their ratio, vary throughout the movements?
